📖 Tournament Format

Day 1 – The Tradition · Pinehurst ($50)

Two-man teams (A+D player pairing). Modified alternate shot — both players drive, hit each other's drives, then alternate from the best ball. On Par 3s: each player drives, then alternate from there. The player whose ball is NOT selected hits next.

Points: 1st: 8pts + $25 each · 2nd: 6pts · 3rd: 4pts · 4th: 2pts per player

Day 2 – Heritage · Yellow Ball ($100)

Two foursomes. The highest handicap player starts with the yellow ball, which rotates every hole from highest to lowest handicap. Each hole score = yellow ball player's gross score + lowest individual net score of other players. Each team gets 3 yellow balls — 2 strokes off for each ball returned at the end.

Points: Winning foursome: 8pts + $25 each · Losing foursome: 4pts each

Day 3 – True Blue · Sixes Match Play ($150)

Match play within each foursome with teams switching every six holes. Net score. Each player earns 3pts per six-hole match won. A push earns 1pt per player. Top point earners split $150 (9pts wins all; if two players tie at 9, they split).

Day 4 – Caledonia · Stroke Play

Net best score. Top 4 players by overall points play together. 8pts to the winner, cascading to 1pt for last place. Player with the most total points wins the 2026 PII Championship and $400.

⚡ Special Events

  • Ace — Wins the ENTIRE pot ($800). Ace also buys dinner at Frank's.
  • Barkie — Par or better after hitting a tree: $10
  • Ferret — Holing out from off the green for par or better: $5
  • Sharkie — Par or better after going in the water: $10
  • Anyone Pool — $100 pool, pays out until spent
